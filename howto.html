HowTo

Tinkraft allows to make blog posts on their platform from inside the game client itself!
You can embed on your posts portions of scenary, images and youtube videos!

Instructions

  • To make a post you first make you have the following: a chest to store the "posts" and an unwritten book. You can reutilize the same chest but you will need a book for each additional post you want to make. You can have as many books and chests as you see fit.
  • If you want to put embed a portion of scenary, you will need to choose something you want to show!

  • Press F3 to show the debug screen, here you can see your position in the world, this is useful to get the coordinates necessary to embed a portion of the scenary. Look at the block section, you will need those.

  • On the first page of your book you can write the following tags:
    • [project *projectname* ]: several posts can be associated if they're put in the same project. this tag is obligatory
    • [cover *url* ]: the cover url will be dispayed on the home
    • [xyz *xa* *ya* *za* *xb* *yb* *zb* ]: this indicates the section of the world you want to display!
  • You can use the rest of the pages of the book to fill up your post! Tell us your adventures, or describe how you built your amazing redstone contraption.

  • Once done, sign your book and choose a title (it will be used for the post title). and store the book inside a chest. In some minutes your post will automatically be submitted.
